Each transformation could have one or more mood tags separate from categories.

Examples:

cute, chaotic, elegant, spooky, glamour, silly, soft, weird, bossy, tiny

Then commands could later allow:

/transform me mood:cute
/transform me mood:spooky
/dailytransform mood:glamour

This is different from categories. Category is “what form”. Mood is “what vibe”. A doll can be cute, spooky, elegant, or glamour. That gives more variety without needing hundreds of new forms.

Why it is good: players choose the tone, not just the object/creature type.
Risk: too many tags will become messy. Keep the list small.
