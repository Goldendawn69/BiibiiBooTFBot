Form Quirks

Every transformation could optionally have 2 or 3 quirks. The bot randomly picks one when the transformation happens.

Example for Living Plush Bunny:

“Your paws make every serious gesture look adorable.”
“You squeak when startled.”
“You keep trying to sit neatly on cushions.”

Example for Boutique Mannequin:

“You freeze dramatically whenever someone compliments your outfit.”
“You keep posing like someone is adjusting a display window.”
“You are oddly offended by bad lighting.”

Why it is good: repeat transformations feel less repetitive.
Spicy-but-safe angle: glamour forms can have confident, flirty, stylish quirks without explicit content.
