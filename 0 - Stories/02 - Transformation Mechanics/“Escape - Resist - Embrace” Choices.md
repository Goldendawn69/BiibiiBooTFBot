After a transformation, users could choose a reaction.

Buttons:

Resist
Embrace
Pose
Panic
Act Natural

Each gives a little response and maybe adjusts future flavour.

Example:

Becci becomes a Boutique Mannequin.

Buttons:
Resist: “You try to move normally. The pose wins for exactly three seconds.”
Embrace: “You settle into the display with worrying elegance.”
Pose: “The lighting improves. Nobody knows how.”
Panic: “A very stylish panic, frankly.”
Act Natural: “You fail. But gracefully.”

This is very fun and very Discord-friendly.

It gives the player agency immediately after the bot does something silly.
