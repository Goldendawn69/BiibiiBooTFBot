Each current form could have a stability meter.

Stable forms last longer.
Unstable forms trigger quirks.
Low stability causes random modifiers.

Example:

Current Form: Living Plush Bunny
Stability: Wobbly
Effect: small chance of “Extra Fluffy” modifier.

This gives the bot game-feel without heavy stats.

But do not overdo it. A simple label is enough:

Stable
Wobbly
Chaotic
Cursed
