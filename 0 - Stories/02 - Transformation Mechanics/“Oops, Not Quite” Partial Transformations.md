A command or event that applies only a small partial change, not a full new form.

Examples:

“Bunny ears for one hour.”
“Porcelain doll blush until the next event.”
“Clockwork tail that ticks when annoyed.”
“Glittery voice effect.”
“Tiny ghostly teacup orbit.”

This gives you softer roleplay hooks without always resetting the whole current form.

Why it is good: it makes the bot feel less binary.
NSFW-safe fun: “glamour polish”, “dangerously elegant posture”, “too-perfect lipstick” are adult-adjacent without being explicit.
