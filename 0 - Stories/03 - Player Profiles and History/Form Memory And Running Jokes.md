The bot could remember a player’s repeated forms and comment on it.

Examples:

“You have become a plushie 5 times. The Toybox is clearly interested.”
“This is your third spooky form this week. Suspicious.”
“You have avoided object forms entirely. The teapot has noticed.”

This creates personality without needing complex AI.

Command:

/history
/stats

Possible stats:

Most common category
Rare forms found
Favourite form
Longest time transformed
Times turned into object
Times escaped back to base form

People love silly stats.
