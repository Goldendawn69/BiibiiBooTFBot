A player gets a small bingo card of categories or moods to collect.

Example card:

Animal
Object
Doll
Spooky
Tiny
Glamour
Fantasy
Plushie
Robot

When they transform into something matching a square, it marks off.

Reward could be silly only:

“Bingo achieved. You earn the title: Certified Magical Liability.”

Why it is good: gives people a reason to try different categories.
Risk: do not make it grindy. This should feel daft, not like a mobile game cursed by spreadsheets.
