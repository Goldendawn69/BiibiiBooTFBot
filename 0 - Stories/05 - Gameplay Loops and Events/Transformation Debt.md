This one is fun but needs care. If a player avoids transformation for several days, the bot can say their “magic pressure” has built up.

Example:

/form

“Magical pressure: High. Something silly is definitely waiting.”

Then their next transformation could get a bonus modifier or longer text.

Why it is good: creates anticipation.
Keep it optional: do not punish people for not playing. Make it flavour, not obligation.

9. Consent-Based Pair Transformations

You already have Random Pair Event in Social Features. The better version is opt-in pair mechanics.

Example:

/pairtransform request target:@user

The target gets an ephemeral/DM prompt:

“Becci wants to trigger a paired transformation with you. Accept?”

If accepted:

“One becomes a haunted teapot. The other becomes the judgemental teacup that keeps orbiting them.”

Or:

“One becomes a cabaret catwoman. The other becomes her dramatic spotlight assistant.”

Why it is good: social interaction, but consent-protected.
Do not allow forced pair events unless both users have opted into random pair targeting.
