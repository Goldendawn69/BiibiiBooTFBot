You already have the Private Flavour idea, which is one of the strongest foundations. Since the bot is for an R18 server but not explicit, I’d add opt-in intensity settings, not NSFW content.

Possible settings:

off
soft
playful
glamour

I would avoid calling the setting “NSFW”. Use “glamour” or “spicy-safe”. That keeps the bot’s vibe cute and avoids dragging it into content moderation sludge.

Example:

Soft: “You feel a little fuzzier and more cuddly than usual.”
Playful: “Your new instincts keep nudging you toward dramatic poses and silly little reactions.”
Glamour: “The change leaves you feeling poised, polished, and dangerously aware of how well the new look suits you.”

Important rule: keep it agency-safe. No “you must obey”, no explicit sexual detail, no creepy mind override. Players can use it, resist it, or ignore it
