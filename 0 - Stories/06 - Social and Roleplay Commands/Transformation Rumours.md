A social command that generates a silly rumour about someone’s current form.

/rumour user:@Becci

Examples:

“Rumour says Becci’s plush bunny ears twitch whenever someone mentions snacks.”
“Rumour says Becci’s haunted teapot form has started judging mugs by social class.”
“Rumour says Becci’s mannequin form has been standing suspiciously still near the best lighting.”

Why it is good: lightweight, funny, reusable, and uses existing current form.
Consent note: only registered users, and maybe opt-out if people dislike being teased.
