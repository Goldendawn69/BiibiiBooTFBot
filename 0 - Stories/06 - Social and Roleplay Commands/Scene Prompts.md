The bot could help roleplay, not just transform.

After a transformation, it can optionally generate a tiny scene starter.

Example:

“Scene Prompt: You are now small enough to fit inside a teacup. Someone nearby notices the cup is moving.”

Or:

“Scene Prompt: Your mannequin form looks perfect under the nearest light, but your reflection just winked.”

This gives players something to respond to.

Keep it optional:

/scene me

or auto-DM if enabled.

This is better than trying to force people into RP. It gives them a hook.
