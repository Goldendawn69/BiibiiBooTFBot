Give the bot a named in-world voice. Not necessarily Bambi, but some mischievous magical machine, fairy, cursed wardrobe, or clerk.

Example names:

The Transformation Bureau
The Glitter Engine
The Form Registry
The Wardrobe Witch
The Curator

Then event text can say:

“The Curator has reviewed today’s forms and found them insufficiently dramatic.”

Why it is good: makes events feel authored rather than random strings.
My vote: “The Glitter Engine” is cute, but “The Form Registry” is funnier if you want bureaucratic magical nonsense.
