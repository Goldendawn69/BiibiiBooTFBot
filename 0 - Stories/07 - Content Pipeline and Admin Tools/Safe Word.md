Aftercare / Recovery Commands

Commands could be:

/recover
Quiet the form’s mental effects and reset private prompts.

/resetform
Return to base form.

/ground
DM a calming “you are still yourself” message, especially if mental changes are enabled.

/pauseflavour
Temporarily stop private flavour and reinforcement DMs.

That gives people a safety valve. If you are going to play with mental-change flavour, you need the opposite button too. That is not just ethical; it makes players more willing to opt in.
